GTUPEDIA Campus Ambassador

Bring GTUPEDIA to your campus. Help students in your city and college discover exam papers, syllabus, and study resources — while building real experience alongside your degree.

Apply now — Google Form ↗

Why apply?

GTUPEDIA is for students, by students. Campus Ambassadors are the link between their college and the platform — so every branch gets the papers and tools they need before exam season hits.

  • Help your classmates Share GTUPEDIA with students in your department, semester, and college so they spend less time hunting for papers and more time studying.
  • Represent your college Be the GTUPEDIA point of contact at your institute — polytechnic, degree, or management campus anywhere in Gujarat.
  • Build skills that count Community building, outreach, and content feedback look great on a resume, portfolio, or MBA / placement story.
  • Network across GTU Work with students from other colleges and courses who are building the same open resource for everyone.
  • Shape what we build next Tell us which subjects, papers, or resources your campus needs most — ambassadors directly influence GTUPEDIA’s roadmap.
  • Flexible commitment Designed around your schedule. No full-time hours — just genuine effort to spread the word and gather feedback from your peers.

Who can apply?

Any current GTU student — BE, BBA, BCA, Diploma, MBA, or other programmes — from any affiliated college in Gujarat. If you care about helping students find study material faster, you’re a good fit.

How to apply

Fill in the short form with your name, email, course, city, and why you want to join. The team will review applications and get in touch.
